Protect yourself in Vietnam

These vaccinations are recommended for most travellers to Vietnam

Hepatitis A

Essential

Food & water safety

Spread through contaminated food and water. Recommended for all travellers.

Typhoid

Essential

Food & water safety

Recommended for most travellers, especially if visiting rural areas.

Essential

MOSQUITO RISKS

Malaria & Dengue in Vietnam

Malaria risk is low in major cities and coastal resorts but present in rural areas. Dengue fever is a risk nationwide.

Low Risk Areas

Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, Hoi An, Nha Trang, and Phu Quoc.

Risk Areas

Rural highland areas. Antimalarials may be recommended.

Check Your Risk
HEALTH ADVICE

Stay healthy in Vietnam

Essential tips for a safe trip

Street Food

Eat at busy stalls where food is cooked fresh and hot. Avoid raw veg.

Insects

Use 50% DEET repellent. Dengue is common, so bite avoidance is key.

Sun Safety

Strong sun and humidity. Stay hydrated and use high SPF.
