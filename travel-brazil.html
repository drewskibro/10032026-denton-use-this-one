MOSQUITO RISKS

Malaria & Dengue in Brazil

Malaria risk is high in the Amazon basin. Dengue fever is a risk nationwide. Zika and Chikungunya are also present.

Amazon Basin

High risk of malaria. Antimalarials are usually recommended.

Cities & Coast

Rio, São Paulo, and most coastal cities have low malaria risk, but Dengue is present.

HEALTH ADVICE

Stay healthy in Brazil

Essential tips for a safe trip

Food & Water

Drink bottled water only. Avoid ice. Eat freshly cooked food.

Sun Safety

Tropical sun is strong. High SPF, hat, and sunglasses are essential.

Insects

Use 50% DEET repellent. Wear long sleeves/trousers at dawn and dusk.
